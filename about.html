WHO IS BARKHA?

Barkha Agarwal is an award winning NewBorn Photographer based out of New Delhi.

Photography has been her passion for over 15 years. By education, she is an engineer and an MBA. After years of corporate experience, she finally heard her true calling and decided to take her passion full time. She took mentorship from multiple photographers – based in California(US), Perth(Australia) and London(UK) – who are considered the best globally in the genre of New Born photography. Her style of photography is already being appreciated globally. She became the first Indian Photographer to teach on the ‘Belly Baby Summit, U.S.A’ (Most reputed global online platform to learn New Born Photography – Followed by thousands of Photographers across 50+ countries). She got selected thrice to teach on this platform where 14 photographers were selected worldwide, as teachers.

Barkha doesn’t just click pictures – She creates memories for life. She believes that having a baby is the most special moment for any women. She gets true happiness when she holds new born babies in her hands and capture the innocence and cuteness of these tiny bundles of joy.

Barkha has been featured in multiple online and offline media. She regularly does photoshoots of celebrities and influencers. She has built ‘Memories by Barkha’ as a premium brand providing finest new born photography of international stature. She is passionate about doing something new in every photoshoot and also providing a lifetime experience to her clients. She makes sure that young Moms and Dads connect with each other and the baby and immerse themselves in this wonderful experience of capturing their innate emotions.

In her words ‘Its not just a photoshoot. Its an experience of a lifetime’
